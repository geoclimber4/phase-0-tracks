Robot Elevator Algorithm:
-Walk up to elevator
	-If floor desired is above you push the top button
	-Else floor desires is below you push the bottom button
-Enter elevator when doors open and face button console
-Push number/letter of floor desired
-Query other people/robots in elevator for their desired floor:
	-If other numbers are given, push the button matching that number
	-Else do nothing.
-Push door close button
-Do nothing Until number on video screen matches desired floor.
-Exit when door opens.
